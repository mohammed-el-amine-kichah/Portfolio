DevCamp hackathon

December 9, 2023

I'm excited to announce that our team clinched second place at the recent DevCamp hackathon! Our winning project: an AI-powered social media planning website. We developed a web application that uses a fine-tuned GPT model to generate customized social media plans.
Here's a quick overview:

Key Features:
- Customized social media strategies
- AI-generated content calendars and posting schedules
- Platform-specific recommendations
- User-friendly interface for inputting brand details

Tech Stack:
- Backend: Django
- AI: Fine-tuned GPT model
- Frontend: Html Css Javascript

The Challenge:
We aimed to solve the common struggle of creating effective social media strategies for businesses and content creators. Our solution leverages AI to provide tailored, efficient planning.
The Result:
Our innovative approach and solid execution impressed the judges, earning us the second-place spot.
